Our Ministry
The ministries of Church of God Sharjah are driven by a desire to give everyone solid biblical direction for life. We believe it is the Word of God that brings the clarity and direction we need when we find ourselves at difficult crossroads. Our Sunday School Ministry, Youth Sessions, Ladies Meeting, and Special Meetings are geared with this purpose in mind.
