What We Are
Summary History
We are a community of more than 300 members. The revival happening at the Church is nothing short of miraculous. Individuals are consistently being saved, baptized in the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy spirit and receiving the gift of the Holy Spirit. Our services usher in the presence of God with a genuine freedom of worship, and allow you to connect with the spirit of God in an unprecedented way. Our Church not only offers a connection to God, but also acts as a family. Under the direction of our pastors and elders, several ministries have been started to meet the needs of today. Church of God- Sharjah provides something for everyone: a growing and powerful youth group, an outstanding Scripture School program (Beginners to Grade 12), Ladies meetings, monthly zonal groups, charity and outreach ministries, mission stations and much more.
